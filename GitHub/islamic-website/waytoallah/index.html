---Welcome to Islam!---

Islam is the religion of the people who worship God (Allah) alone. The people who worship Allah alone are called Muslims and Muslim means one in submission to God(Allah). We believe in Jesus(Prophet Isa) as being a prophet of God(Allah) and we believe that Prophet Muhammad is the last and final messenger of God(Allah) Muslims worship Allah five times a day with their five daily salat(prayers).
